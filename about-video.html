I Love You

Reason I made this:

We've been together for a year. THAT's 12 whole months; 356 Days and counting! Over the course of the year, we've also taken many many pictures. As I thought about someething to do for our Anniversary, I knew I wanted to put something in with a lot of effort. Something I couldn't just buy. I think back to the summer when you said I couldnt make you a website, but its one of the only talents I have, and I wanted to use it to benefit us. So here it is, a website gallery of our couples pictures. They'll always be here, no matter what. If there's a new phone, no storage, lost iPad, whatever, our pictures will live here forever.

Remember

You're my other half.
